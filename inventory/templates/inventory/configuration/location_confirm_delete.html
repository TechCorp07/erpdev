Safe Deletion Guidelines

Safe to delete when:

  • No inventory stored at location
  • No storage bins configured
  • Location is inactive for extended period
  • Created by mistake or duplicate

Avoid deletion when:

  • Contains inventory or stock records
  • Has storage bins with items
  • Currently active in operations
  • Historical movements exist
  • Audit compliance requires records
