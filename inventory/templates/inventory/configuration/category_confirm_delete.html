Safe Deletion Guidelines

Safe to delete when:

  • No products assigned to category
  • No subcategories exist
  • Category was created by mistake
  • Category is duplicate or obsolete

Avoid deletion when:

  • Products or subcategories exist
  • Category has business rules configured
  • Used in filtering or reporting
  • Part of important hierarchy
