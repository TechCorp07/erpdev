Deletion Guidelines

Safe to delete when:

  • No products assigned to supplier
  • No purchase order history
  • No outstanding financial obligations
  • Supplier was created by mistake

Avoid deletion when:

  • Products reference this supplier
  • Historical purchase orders exist
  • Financial obligations are pending
  • Auditing/compliance requires records
