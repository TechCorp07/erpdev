PCB Fabrication

PCB fabrication is the process of making printed circuit boards that are used on all electronic devices internationally e.g. laptops, cell phones, remote controls etc. The company designs and develops PCBs for security systems and for local companies that outsource their designs to China for fabrication at a fraction of the current expensive cost.

This localizing of production benefits the country's GDP as it creates local employment and reduces outsourcing of manufacturing costs. Our PCB fabrication services include:

  • Custom PCB Design
  • Prototype Development
  • Component Sourcing
  • Production Setup
  • Quality Testing
  • Repair & Maintenance
