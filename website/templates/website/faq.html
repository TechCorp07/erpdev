For bulk orders or special requirements, we recommend contacting us directly to discuss your needs.

Security Systems

We offer a comprehensive range of security systems including biometric access control, surveillance cameras, web cameras, motion detection systems, real-time monitoring solutions, and cyber security services. Our systems can be customized to meet the specific security needs of residential homes, office premises, and large industrial complexes.

Yes, we provide complete installation services for all our security systems. Our team of trained technicians handles the entire process from initial site assessment to final setup and testing. We also offer maintenance services to ensure your security systems continue to function optimally. Regular maintenance packages are available to keep your systems up-to-date and in good working condition.

Yes, our security systems are designed to be flexible and can be integrated with your existing security infrastructure. Whether you're looking to upgrade certain components or add new features to your current system, our solutions can be tailored to work seamlessly with what you already have. This approach allows for cost-effective security enhancements without the need for a complete system overhaul.

IoT & Smart Solutions

We offer a wide range of IoT devices and systems including smart thermostats, smart meters, smart locks, connected appliances, and robotic equipment. Our IoT solutions are designed to enhance automation, improve efficiency, and provide seamless connectivity for homes and businesses.

Our IoT solutions provide businesses with improved operational efficiency, real-time monitoring capabilities, data-driven insights, enhanced security, and cost savings. By automating routine tasks and providing actionable data, businesses can make more informed decisions, optimize resource allocation, and improve overall productivity.

Yes, security is a top priority in all our IoT implementations. PCB Fabrication

We provide comprehensive PCB fabrication services including design, prototype development, component sourcing, production setup, quality testing, and repair & maintenance. Our PCB fabrication services cater to various industries and applications, from security systems to custom electronic devices.

Local PCB fabrication offers several advantages including faster turnaround times, reduced shipping costs, better communication, easier quality control, customization flexibility, and support for the local economy. By choosing our local PCB fabrication services, you're contributing to job creation and skill development within Zimbabwe while receiving high-quality products tailored to your specific needs.

Our PCB production capacity can accommodate both small-batch prototyping and larger production runs. We have the capability to handle various PCB specifications including single-layer, double-layer, and multi-layer boards with different thicknesses and materials. For specific capacity requirements or large-scale production inquiries, please contact us directly to discuss your project needs.

Power Systems

We offer a range of power system solutions including electrical wiring for office and residential spaces, solar water pumps, solar lighting kits, wind water pumps, low noise generators, and renewable energy R&D services. Our power systems are designed to provide reliable and efficient energy solutions for various applications.

Yes, our solar power systems are designed to be cost-effective in the long run. While there is an initial investment, solar systems typically pay for themselves over time through reduced electricity bills. We offer various financing options to make the initial investment more manageable, and our systems are designed for durability and efficiency to maximize your return on investment.

Most of our power systems require minimal maintenance. Solar panels generally need occasional cleaning to ensure optimal performance, and batteries may need replacement after several years of use. We provide maintenance guidelines with all our installations and offer maintenance service packages to ensure your systems continue to operate efficiently throughout their lifespan.

Orders & Payment

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, mobile money services (EcoCash, OneMoney), and major credit/debit cards. For online purchases, we also support secure online payment options. For large orders or project-based services, we can discuss flexible payment arrangements.

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the items ordered. For in-stock items within Harare, we typically deliver within 1-2 business days. For other locations in Zimbabwe, delivery usually takes 2-5 business days. For custom or large orders, delivery times will be communicated during the ordering process. We work with reliable courier services to ensure prompt and secure delivery.

Yes, we offer warranties on our products and services. The warranty period varies depending on the specific product or service, with most electronic components covered by a standard 90-day warranty and larger systems like solar installations typically covered for 1-2 years. Extended warranty options are available for most products. All warranty details are provided at the time of purchase.
