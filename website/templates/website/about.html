About BlitzTech Electronics

We are a global tech intermediary that facilitates seamless connections between the African business and consumer sectors and the international marketplace.

Our mission is to enhance economic collaboration and value creation across borders through technology-enabled interactions.

Our Vision

Elevation of our clients' business and lives through the power of custom-fit solutions.

We envision a Zimbabwe where technological innovation drives economic growth and creates opportunities for all. Our aim is to be at the forefront of this transformation, providing solutions that are both cutting-edge and accessible.

Our Mission

To become the leading innovative electronic systems developers in Zimbabwe by exceeding customers' quality expectations through delivery of transcendent, tailor-made services of unstinting high quality and value addition.

We aim to bridge the gap between supply and demand in electronics, making components and technology accessible throughout Zimbabwe while supporting local innovation.

Company Facts

Our Beginnings

BlitzTech Electronics was launched in August 2017 with a vision to revolutionize the electronics industry in Zimbabwe. By August 2018, we had gained a market share of about 50% among students of local tertiary institutes specializing in electronics-related programs.

Our growth has been exceptional, with a year-over-year revenue increase of at least 300% through smart partnerships with global giants like Arduino, Hikvision, Centurion, and Microchip.
